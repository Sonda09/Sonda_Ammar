About Me Get to Know me.
I'm an Assistant Professor in Computer Science based in Sfax , Tunisia
I obtained my diploma in Computer Science and Multimedia in 2009, my master's degree in Information Processing Systems and Multimedia from Rouen University, Rouen, France, in 2010, and my Ph.D. in Computer Systems Engineering in 2014 from the National Engineering School of Sfax - Tunisia. Currently, I'm an assistant professor at the Enet-Com and a member of the SM@RTS Laboratory. My research focuses on information analysis, and fusion at various levels, uncertain information modeling, data and information quality, small data analysis, and feature selection. My research works are applied to obstacle detection and recognition. Since 2011, I have been leading a research team working on a smart white cane project.
